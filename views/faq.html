Frequently Asked Questions

You ask and we answer

What services does MediLab provide?

We offer a wide range of diagnostic and medical services, including blood tests, imaging, cardiology, neurology, pediatrics, eye care, and more.

How can I book an appointment?

Appointments can be scheduled online or by calling our clinic. Some doctors are currently available for online booking.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring a valid ID, any previous medical records, and a list of medications you are currently taking.

Are test results confidential?

Yes, all patient information and test results are handled with the strictest confidentiality and privacy standards.

How long does it take to get test results?

Most routine test results are available within 24–48 hours. Specialized tests may take longer, and our staff will inform you of the expected timeline.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, MediLab accepts a wide range of insurance plans. Please check with our staff or your provider to confirm coverage.
