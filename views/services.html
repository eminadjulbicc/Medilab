Services

Cardiology

Comprehensive heart care including check-ups, diagnostics, and treatment for all cardiovascular conditions, ensuring your heart stays healthy.

Expert diagnosis and treatment of neurological disorders affecting the brain, spine, and nervous system, with personalized care plans for every patient.

Full range of ophthalmology services including routine vision exams, treatment of eye diseases, and advanced vision correction procedures.

Accurate and fast diagnostic testing with modern lab equipment, including blood tests, pathology, and specialized diagnostics for comprehensive health assessments.
